DISCREET TECHNOLOGY

Introducing the world’s thinnest wearable. The Chronos comes in at under 3mm (about the thickness of a nickel), so it won’t interfere with the design or feel of your watch.

ADAPTABLE DESIGN

Clever micro-suction technology easily attaches and detaches to your watches. No sticky adhesives, no residues, and no magnets to interfere with your treasured timepieces. (Use our sizing app to find out if it fits your watch.)

CONNECTED, NOT DISTRACTED

Smart vibration and light notifications filter out the noise and alert you to just the essentials. Control your phone with simple taps and gestures that silence calls, skip music tracks, sync to your connected home and more.

THE DISC

The Chronos measures under 3 mm thin and 33 mm in diameter to fit to most round-faced watches.

  • Stainless steel casing
  • Bluetooth 4.0 Low Energy integration
  • Accelerometer technology
  • Haptic vibration and LED notifications
  • Non-residue micro-suction adhesive backing
  • Waterproof construction
  • 36 hour battery life
  • Wireless charging
  • Chronos disc fits on over 80% of watches sold today
