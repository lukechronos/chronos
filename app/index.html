DISCREET TECHNOLOGY

The world’s thinnest wearable, Chronos comes in at under 3mm thin, so it won’t interfere with the design or feel of your watch.

ADAPTABLE DESIGN

Clever micro-suction technology easily attaches and detaches to your watches. That means no sticky adhesives, no residues, and no magnets.

CONNECTED, NOT DISTRACTED

Control your phone with simple taps and gestures that silence calls, skip music tracks, sync to your connected home and more.

THE DISC

Chronos measures under 3mm thin and 33mm in diameter to fit most watches.

Every Chronos is equipped with:

  • Stainless steel casing
  • Bluetooth 4.0 Low Energy integration
  • Waterproof construction
  • 36 hour battery life
  • Wireless charging
  • Accelerometer technology
  • Haptic vibration and LED notifications
  • Non-residue micro-suction adhesive backing
  • Fits on over 80% of watches sold today
  • Patent pending design
